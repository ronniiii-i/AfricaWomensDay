About the Africa Women's Day Event 2022

Although Africa Women's Day has been in existence since 1962, few people know about this important day. Last year 2021, we focused on past and present Africa women presidents and prime ministers since independence and their unrivaled contribution to the growth of Africa as a continent. We were able to improve on the lack of awareness, reaching and engaging over 10,000 Africans globally and to give the political legacy of African Women the spotlight and honor it deserves. We believe stepping up this campaign with the right narrative is the only healthy way to communicate our history to the younger generation of Africa women and women.
