Imagine a gender equal world free of bias, stereotypes and discrimination

Africa's Women's Day, July 31st

Africa's Women's Day focuses on spotlighting the legacy of African Women and aims to honor each of them for their visionary, bold, and pioneering historical achievements and contributions to Africa and world history. Because of the peculiar nature of our history, and the roles of the African woman in it, it is important to raise awareness of the importance and existence of Africa's Women's Day. Every July 31, we get to celebrate Africa Women's Day. Women have had a long history of oppression, but every fight gets us one step closer to freedom and equality. A world where difference is valued and celebrated.Together we can forge women's equality.Collectively we can all Embrace Equity.Lets celebrate women's achievement.Raise awareness about discrimination. Take action to drive gender parity. AWD belongs to everyone, everywhere. Inclusion means all AWD action is valid.

African Woman's Day 2023 Mission Statement

Under the inspiring theme "Empowered Woman, Empowered Africa", our mission for African Woman's Day 2023 is to spotlight and celebrate the remarkable African women founders and CEOs who are shaping the future of the continent. We aim to amplify their journeys, achievements, and contributions, demonstrating how their empowerment has led to a stronger, more vibrant Africa. Our focus will be on the catalytic role these leaders play in driving economic growth, fostering innovation, and creating sustainable development. By sharing their stories, we hope to inspire the next generation of African female leaders, innovators, and change-makers. We are dedicated to promoting the importance of gender equality, empowerment, and leadership for women. We believe that an empowered African woman is a cornerstone for an empowered, prosperous Africa. As we shine a light on these powerful women, we reaffirm our commitment to dismantling barriers and creating more opportunities for women's leadership across all sectors. Together, let's celebrate the power of African women because when women rise, Africa rises.

African Women's Day

Africa's Women's Day (AWD) is just around the corner on the 31st of July!As part of our annual tradition, throughout the entire month of July, we will be shining a spotlight on the achievements of inspiring African women! This year, in 2023, we proudly celebrate the exceptional Female Founders and CEOs who have made their mark on the continent.The theme for this year's AWD is "Empowered Women, Empowered Africa," encapsulating the driving force behind our efforts to promote gender equality and women's empowerment in Africa. It serves as a powerful reminder of the positive impact that empowered women can have on the continent's progress and prosperity.In support of this awareness campaign, AWD has joined forces with GirlsInTech Nigeria, a dynamic organization working tirelessly to empower and uplift women in the technology industry. Together, we aim to highlight the incredible contributions of female leaders, inspire the next generation of women entrepreneurs, and foster an inclusive and supportive environment for women's success.

In our pursuit of a truly impactful event, we are delighted to announce our partnership with GirlsInTech NigeriaA registered non-profit organization dedicated to empowering women and girls in the tech industry. Together, we aim to harness the potential of technology and showcase the remarkable work of these young women, illustrating the profound connection between women's empowerment and the progress of our beloved continent. In our efforts to endorse the commendable initiatives undertaken by GirlsInTech Nigeria, we are actively seeking donations to support their mission of empowering African women in the field of technology. Your generous contributions will be immensely valued as we strive towards this noble cause. Kindly Donate Here. We extend a warm invitation to all Sponsors, Partners, and Donors to join us in supporting this initiative. Together, let's celebrate the accomplishments of African Female Founders and CEOs, acknowledge the strength and resilience of women across the continent, and pave the way for a brighter, more empowered future for all. Join us in making this year's Africa's Women's Day a resounding success!
