AFRICA'S WOMEN'S DAY - JULY 31, 2023

Imagine a gender equal world free of
bias, stereotypes and discrimination

On July 31, 2023 we get to celebrate Africa Women's Day.

Women have had a long history of oppression, but every fight gets us one step closer to freedom and equality.
A world where difference is valued and celebrated. Together we can forge women's equality. Collectively we can all EmbraceEquity.

Lets celebrate women's achievement. Raise awareness about discrimination.
Take action to drive gender parity.
AWD belongs to everyone, everywhere. Inclusion means all AWD action is valid.

Africa Women's Day 2023 Missions

AWD sees a number of MISSION to help forge a gender equal world. Celebrating
women's achievements and increasing visibility, while calling out inequality, is key.
